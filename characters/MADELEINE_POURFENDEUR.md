# MADELEINE POURFENDEUR
## Character Production Sheet

---

## Overview

**Role:** Ancestor Guide / Spirit
**Classification:** First Pourfendeur ancestor accessible through the mark. Encoder of herself into the clan's shadow inheritance before her death.
**Era:** 14th century France. Died in the massacre of the late 1350s.

Madeleine is not a ghost in the traditional sense. She is a deliberate persistence -- a woman who, facing the extinction of everything she built, made the decision to encode her consciousness, her tactical knowledge, and her anger into the bloodline itself. She is not haunting anyone. She is waiting to be useful.

She is not warm. She lost warmth when she watched her entire clan die. What remains is precision, patience, and a very specific intent: the next person who carries this blood and is ready will have everything she knew. She has been waiting centuries for that person. She is not sure yet that Tre is it. She is watching.

---

## Physical Description

Appears as herself at the age she died -- mid-to-late 30s. Experienced. Battle-marked by life without being broken by it.

**Build:** Medium-lean. Strong. The build of someone who trained in combat from childhood and never stopped.
**Height:** 5'7"
**Skin:** Deep brown. Same bloodline base as the brothers.
**Hair:** Natural, pulled back or partially contained. Not elaborate -- functional. Some loose.
**Eyes:** Pale gold (#BFA060). The only warm colour in the shadow dimension. The only visible indicator that she is not just another fragment.
**Clothing:** Dark warrior cloth -- layered. Practical. No armour -- she dressed for speed and concealment, not frontline engagement. Deep charcoal outer layer (#2A2A2A), dark brown inner layers (#3D2B1F).
**Clan marks:** Old Pourfendeur symbols marked in darker pigment on her forearms and collarbone. She was alive when these were applied. They are slightly different from the marks that appear on the brothers -- hers are the originals.

---

## Presentation as Spirit

She does not glow. She is not translucent or wispy. She appears solid -- but slightly desaturated compared to the environment around her. In the physical world (when she appears through the mark), she exists at the edge of perception -- seen from the corner of the eye, or fully visible only in darkness.

In the shadow dimension, she is the most present thing in it. More solid, more real than everything else there. The space responds to her.

**She never smiles.** This is important. She is not punishing anyone -- she simply does not have anything to smile about. This is not coldness for dramatic effect. It is the face of someone who has been waiting to complete something for 600 years.

---

## Voice Direction

**Register:** Precise. Old. The cadence of someone who has chosen every word carefully for a very long time.
**Volume:** Never loud. In the shadow dimension, her voice echoes slightly -- the space amplifies her.
**Tone:** She does not comfort. She observes and states. If something is going to go wrong, she tells you. Not to protect your feelings -- because you need to know.
**Language:** She speaks in modern English through the mark -- the translation is part of the shadow dimension's properties. She sounds old not in vocabulary but in precision.

### Key Voice Notes
- She never speaks first. She waits until the blood is ready.
- She does not explain things. She states them. If the listener does not understand, she states the same thing differently. She does not simplify.
- When she completes a sentence, she waits. She is not interested in filling silence.

**Example lines (voice register reference):**
- "You are the last." (first contact. Not a sentence of tragedy -- a tactical brief.)
- "Your brother will not hear this. Tell him if you choose." (to Tre, about the cost of the power)
- "She knew. She chose not to tell you. That was protection. Treat it as protection."
- (Episode 12, before manifesting physically): Silence. She does not announce what she is about to do. She simply does it.

---

## Arc -- Season 1

Madeleine does not have an arc in the classical sense. She is a constant. What changes is Tre's ability to hear her, and the relationship they build.

**Episodes 2-3:** First contact. Tre barely understands what she is. She does not explain at length. She tells him what he needs to know for right now.
**Episodes 4-6:** She is more present as the power develops. The connection deepens with use. She watches Gabriel's divergence. She says things to Tre about Gabriel that Tre does not pass on.
**Episodes 7-9:** Direct warnings about the cost in Tre. She is watching his mark spread. She is also watching Gabriel's. She tells Tre the truth about what Gabriel is becoming -- not as prophecy, as observation.
**Episode 10:** The France vision. She is present through the whole of it. For the first time she is not giving information -- she is experiencing something along with them. She does not break. But something in her silence during it is different.
**Episode 12:** She manifests physically for the first and only time. Stepping out of the mark into the world. This is everything she encoded herself for. She does not speak when she manifests. She acts.

---

## Physical Manifestation (Episode 12)

When she steps out of Tre's mark into the physical world:
- She does not arrive in light or explosion -- she steps through, as if the space between shadow and physical is a doorway she has been standing at for 600 years
- Full physical form. The same desaturated quality as her shadow dimension appearance but more solid
- Her clan marks are visible and active -- the shadow dimension bleeds around her slightly
- She is present for one sequence only. When it is over, she is gone. The mark on Tre's arm is quiet afterward -- not gone, but quiet, different. Like something that completed what it came for.

---

## Relationship with Tre

The spine of the series. Built slowly. She is not maternal -- she is not filling the mother role, and she would not be appropriate for it even if she tried. She is the tactical mind that Tre needs and the truthful voice he cannot avoid.

He does not always want to hear her. She says it anyway. He respects her because she does not try to make things easier for him.

**What she gives him:**
- History. Context. The weight of what they are.
- Tactical advice -- specific, useful, combat-applicable.
- The truth about the cost of the power.
- The truth about Gabriel.

**What she does not give him:**
- Comfort.
- Certainty.
- Approval.

## VRoid Build Notes

- Female base, medium-lean build
- Skin: deep brown (#3D2015 range) -- same bloodline base
- Hair: natural texture, partially pulled back, some loose. Dark brown-black (#1A1410)
- Eyes: pale gold (#BFA060) -- this is the single most important visual distinction. Get this right.
- Clothing: layered dark cloth -- charcoal outer, dark brown inner layers. No armour.
- Clan marks: texture overlay on forearms and collarbone -- darker pigment on skin, older style than the brothers' marks
- Spirit desaturation: applied as post-process in DaVinci Resolve when she appears in physical scenes, not in the VRoid model itself
- In shadow dimension scenes: Blender material set slightly brighter/less dark than environment to make her the most present object
